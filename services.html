02 — Influencer Marketing
Your brand, in the right hands, in front of the right audience.

Strategic creator partnerships that put your brand in front of an engaged, relevant audience. We handle the entire process: finding the right creators, negotiating the deal, briefing the content and tracking the performance.

01
Creator Identification & Vetting
We research and identify creators whose audience genuinely matches yours, not just on follower count, but on engagement quality, content style and audience demographics. No spray-and-pray approach.
02
Outreach & Deal Negotiation
We manage the outreach and negotiate the partnership terms on your behalf, including rates, deliverables, timelines and usage rights. You get the deal you want without the back-and-forth.
03
Brief Writing & Creative Direction
A clear brief that gives creators the context and guidelines they need to produce content that's on-brand, without stifling their voice. The balance between creative freedom and brand consistency is everything.
04
Campaign Management & Reporting
We track the content, log the performance data and deliver a clear post-campaign report covering reach, impressions, engagement, conversions and what we'd do differently next time.

01
Campaign Strategy & Concept
We start with the objective, the audience and the budget, then build a campaign concept that's distinctive, deliverable and commercially focused. Not ideas for the sake of ideas.
02
Creative Production
Ad creatives, social content, landing page copy and email sequences, everything the campaign needs to run. Produced in-house and built around the platform and the audience.
03
Paid Media Management
Paid social and paid search across Meta, Google, LinkedIn and TikTok. We set up, run and optimise your ads to hit the performance targets we agreed at the start. No set-and-forget.
04
Campaign Reporting & Analysis
A full debrief at campaign end covering what worked, what didn't, what it cost and what it generated. A clear picture of return on investment and what we'd iterate on next time.
